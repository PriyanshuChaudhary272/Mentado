About Us.

The Mentado, founded in Feb 2022, is an online counseling platform, catering to the unique mental health needs of students. We strive to create a safe space by allowing individuals to tell their story in a supportive, non-judgmental environment.
​ We firmly believe that mental health therapy is universally beneficial  to Anyone & Everyone. The uses of therapy extend far beyond addressing human crisis and tragedies; it enables individuals to tap into their core personality and wisdom to thrive.
